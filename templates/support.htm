Frequently Asked

Where is my order?

Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking email within 24 hours. Orders typically arrive in 3–7 business days within Pakistan.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day hassle-free return window. Items must be unworn and in original packaging with all tags attached.

Is my watch covered under warranty?

Every Hero Watch comes with a 2-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects. Accidental damage is not included.

How do I change the watch strap?

All Hero straps use a quick-release spring bar system. A guide video is included with your order, or visit our YouTube channel.
